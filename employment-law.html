About Employment Law

Whether you're an employee facing workplace issues or an employer seeking to comply with labor laws, Huntsville's employment attorneys provide expert guidance. They help protect rights and resolve disputes in Alabama's dynamic job market.

Common Services

  • Workplace discrimination claims
  • Sexual harassment cases
  • Wrongful termination
  • Employment contracts and non-competes
  • Wage and hour disputes
  • FMLA and leave issues
  • Whistleblower protection
  • HR policy development
  • EEOC claims and investigations

For Employees

If you've experienced discrimination, harassment, or unfair treatment at work, you have legal rights. Employment attorneys help workers understand their options, file complaints with appropriate agencies, and pursue claims against employers who violate labor laws.

For Employers

Huntsville employers must navigate complex federal and state employment regulations. Local attorneys help businesses develop compliant policies, handle employee disputes, and defend against claims while maintaining productive workplace environments.
