About Immigration Law

Huntsville's aerospace and technology industries attract talent from around the world, making immigration law services essential to our community. Local immigration attorneys help individuals, families, and employers navigate the complex U.S. immigration system.

Immigration Services

  • Work visas (H-1B, L-1, O-1, TN)
  • Green card applications
  • Family-based immigration
  • Naturalization and citizenship
  • Employment-based immigration
  • Asylum and refugee cases
  • Deportation defense
  • DACA renewals
  • Investor visas (EB-5)

Supporting Huntsville's Global Workforce

With major employers like NASA, defense contractors, and tech companies seeking international talent, Huntsville immigration attorneys help both employers and employees with work authorization, permanent residency applications, and compliance with immigration regulations.

Family Immigration

Immigration attorneys also assist with family reunification, helping U.S. citizens and permanent residents bring family members to the country legally. They guide families through the petition process, visa applications, and adjustment of status procedures.
