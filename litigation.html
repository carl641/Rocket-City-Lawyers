About Litigation

When disputes can't be resolved through negotiation, skilled litigators are essential. Huntsville's litigation attorneys represent clients in state and federal courts, administrative proceedings, and alternative dispute resolution forums throughout North Alabama.

Litigation Services

  • Civil litigation and trials
  • Commercial disputes
  • Appeals and appellate practice
  • Arbitration and mediation
  • Contract disputes
  • Insurance litigation
  • Class action defense
  • Administrative hearings
  • Judgment enforcement

Experienced Trial Advocacy

Effective litigation requires thorough preparation, strategic thinking, and persuasive courtroom skills. Huntsville litigators have extensive experience before Madison County Circuit Court, the Northern District of Alabama federal court, and the Alabama appellate courts.

Alternative Dispute Resolution

Not every dispute needs to go to trial. Experienced litigators also handle arbitration and mediation, helping clients resolve conflicts more efficiently while still protecting their interests and achieving favorable outcomes.
